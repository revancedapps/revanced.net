ReVanced Twitch Features

  • Automatic Channel Point claiming: Automatically claims Channel Points as they become available, so you don't have to worry about it.
  • Audio ad blocking: Blocks audio ads in streams and VODs, so you can enjoy your content without interruption.
  • Embedded stream ad blocking: Blocks embedded stream ads from services like TTV.lol and PurpleAdBlocker, so you don't have to see them.
  • Video ad blocking: Blocks video ads in streams and VODs, so you can watch your content without having to skip through them.
  • Twitch debugging mode: Enables Twitch's internal debugging mode, which can be useful for troubleshooting issues.
  • Settings menu: Adds a settings menu to Twitch, where you can configure various features of ReVanced Twitch.
  • Deleted chat messages: Shows deleted chat messages behind a clickable spoiler, so you can still see what was said even if it was deleted.
