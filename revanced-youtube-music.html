ReVanced YouTube Music

ReVanced YouTube Music is a modified version of the official YouTube Music app that offers a number of premium features for free, including background playback, ad-free listening, and higher audio quality. It is a great option for users who want to enjoy all the features of YouTube Music without having to pay for a premium subscription.

ReVanced YouTube Music Features

  • Background playback: Enables you to listen to music even when the app is closed.
  • Android Auto support: Allows you to use YouTube Music on Android Auto without any problems.
  • Audio codec options: Lets you choose from a variety of audio codecs, for better audio quality.
  • No music category bar: Removes the music category bar from the homepage, for a cleaner look.
  • Video-free playback: Enables you to play music without the video, saving data and battery.
  • No "Get Premium" ads: Removes all the "Get Premium" ads from the app, for a more pleasant experience.
  • Minimized playback on Kids music: Lets you minimize YouTube Music while playing Kids music, for easy multitasking.
  • Ad-free music player: Removes all ads from the music player, for a distraction-free listening experience.
  • No upgrade tab: Removes the "Upgrade to Premium" tab from the pivot bar, for a cleaner look.
  • Rootless and package name change: Allows you to use YouTube Music ReVanced without root and under a different package name, for more flexibility.
