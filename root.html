How to Install?

You cannot install non-root version APK on a rooted Android device.

  • Download the official installer from the GDrive link found in below. 1
  • Download the suitable APK file for your device architecture from the table given below
    (check architecture using CPU-Z app free download from Play Store) 2
  • Move the downloaded APK file into /sdcard/Downloads or inside the installation zip file. 3
  • Go to the Play Store and uninstall all YouTube updates and disable auto updated option. 4
  • Reboot into the custom recovery mode such as TWRP. 5
  • Flash the installer through TWRP. 6

Remember to uninstall YouTube updates and disable auto update option found in Play Store. If you don't do this, updated app will replace the ReVanced app automatically and you will not see any new features.
