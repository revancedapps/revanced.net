YouTube ReVanced for iOS

YouTube ReVanced has become very popular for its various integrated tweaks, such as adblocking, background playback, sponsor ads block, and auto-repeat. However, it is only available for Android devices, leaving iOS users wondering if they can also have it.

Unfortunately, the team behind ReVanced has not yet developed an iOS version, so downloading ReVanced for iOS is not possible . at the moment.

But...
there is an alternative app that can offer similar features to ReVanced.

Alternative For YouTube ReVanced For iOS

For Apple iOS users, the best alternative to ReVanced is YouTube++. It's a modified version of the original YouTube app, although it doesn't offer all the same features as ReVanced.
However, when looking at its features, YouTube++ is currently the best alternative available for iOS users looking for a similar experience to YouTube ReVanced.

YouTube++ Features:

  • Ad-blocking: YouTube++ blocks all types of ads, including video ads and pop-up ads, so you can enjoy uninterrupted watching.
  • Background playback: You can listen to YouTube videos in the background even when the app is closed or your device is locked.
  • Download videos: You can download any video from YouTube to your device's camera roll for offline viewing.
  • Audio-only mode: You can listen to the audio of a video without having to watch the video, which is useful for listening to music or podcasts.
  • No age restrictions: YouTube++ removes all age restrictions on videos, allowing you to watch any content without any limitations.
  • Customization: You can customize the interface of YouTube++ with different themes, backgrounds, and colors.
  • Speed control: You can adjust the playback speed of a video to slow it down or speed it up, depending on your preference.
  • Repeat mode: You can set a video to repeat automatically, which is useful for playing music videos or guided meditations.
  • Enhanced sharing: You can share videos directly from YouTube++ to social media platforms, email, or messaging apps.
  • No jailbreak required: You can install and use YouTube++ on your iOS device without the need for a jailbreak.

Download and Install YouTube++

For Non-Jailbroken Devices

To install YouTube++ on a non-jailbroken device, you'll need a sideload utility like Cydia Impactor or AltStore.
If you choose to use AltStore, you can follow these simple instructions to sideload YouTube++:

  1. First, download and install the AltStore on your iOS device. You can find the installation instructions for AltStore on their official website.
  2. Next, open the Safari browser on your iOS device and go to the YouTube++ page on the iOS Ninja website.
  3. Tap on the "Download" button on the page, and then select "Open in AltStore" from the options that appear.
  4. AltStore will then install YouTube++ on your device, and you'll see the progress in the AltStore app.
  5. Once the installation is complete, go to your device's settings and select "General", then "Profiles & Device Management".
  6. Find the profile for YouTube++ and trust it by tapping on "Trust".
  7. You can now open YouTube++ and start using its features.

Note that you'll need to re-install YouTube++ every seven days as the certificate for the app expires after that period. You can use AltStore's built-in feature to refresh the certificate and continue using YouTube++ without interruption.

For Jailbroken Devices

If your device is jailbroken, follow this guide to simply install YouTube++ with Cydia.

  1. Open Cydia on your jailbroken iOS device.
  2. Tap on the "Sources" tab at the bottom of the screen, and then tap on "Edit" in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap on the "Add" button in the top-left corner, and then type in the URL for the repo that hosts YouTube++. You can use any trusted repo that hosts the app, such as "https://apt.alfhaily.me/". Tap "Add Source" to add the repo.
  4. Once the repo is added, tap on it and search for "YouTube++" in the search bar.
  5. Tap on "YouTube++" in the search results, and then tap "Install" in the top-right corner of the screen.
  6. Cydia will download and install YouTube++ on your device. Once the installation is complete, tap "Restart SpringBoard" to restart your device.
  7. You can now open YouTube++ and start using its features.

Note that jailbreaking your device can void its warranty and may cause security issues. Be sure to follow all precautions and use trusted repos when downloading jailbreak apps.
