What is YouTube ReVanced?

YouTube ReVanced is a modified version of the official YouTube app for Android devices that offers additional features not available in the official app. These features include ad-blocking, background playback, pinch-to-zoom for videos, and more. ReVanced also includes a dark mode option for the app interface.

ReVanced is not available on the Google Play Store and must be downloaded from a third-party source. Users must also have the original YouTube app installed on their device to use ReVanced, as it uses some of the same components as the official app.

ReVanced is not affiliated with or endorsed by Google, and its use may violate YouTube's terms of service. Users should exercise caution and use at their own risk.

Is it available on Google Play Store?

No, YouTube ReVanced is not available on the Google Play Store. It is a modified version of the official YouTube app and does not comply with Google's policies for apps on the Play Store.
